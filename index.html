Consumers often find online reviews helpful in making purchase decisions on e-commerce platforms. Reviews can convey different kinds of information about the product to the reader. The goal of this task is to identify the attributes conveyed by reviews, and classify them into two categories: quality or individual preference.

Effect of User-Uploaded Images - Task Instructions

In this study, you will read a series of online reviews for products. Each review is an evaluation of some product, based on the reviewer’s own experience. These are actual reviews from an actual online marketplace, therefore typos, non-readable special characters, and misspellings are expected. Please read as if you would read actual online reviews. The review will contain only text (both text and pictures). Your task is to classify the contents of each review. The review may contain information on the quality of the product or the personal preferences of that review writer. Definitions and examples are below-

  1. Quality: Quality is an objective measure of the degree of excellence or superiority of a product. Typically, there would be general consensus among consumers reading the review regarding quality attributes. For example, attributes like packaging, price, customer care, durability, efficiency, timeliness of delivery, ease/difficulty of assembly instructions, literal mentions of quality are more likely to reflect quality. You can think of quality attributes as those where more is better, less is worse.
  2. Individual Preference: Any content that reflects the reviewer’s personal taste or preference for the item, which would be applicable for a subset of consumers reading the review. For example, how a product looks (color, pattern, design, attractiveness, and aesthetics) or feels (soft, firm) is more likely to reflect individual preference. For these attributes, people differ in what they like.

Whether the review text describes quality- or taste- related attributes will depend on the context. For example, while sturdiness of a piece of furniture is a quality-related attribute, the firmness of a mattress is subject to individual preferences.

You will be asked to copy and paste sections of the review that you feel reflect quality, and similarly those that reflect individual preference in two separate boxes. You can paste words, phrases or entire sentences. If some sections correspond to both quality and individual preference, you may copy and paste them in both boxes. If you find that no part of the review reflects quality or taste, you may leave the boxes empty.
